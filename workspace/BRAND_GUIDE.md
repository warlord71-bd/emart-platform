## Brand Positioning

Emart Skincare Bangladesh is a locally verified health and beauty business serving Bangladesh with carefully curated authentic global beauty products.

Emart carries Korean beauty first, along with Japanese, UK, USA, France, Thailand, Chinese brand products, Taiwan, EU, aesthetic beauty items, derma cosmetics, beauty tools and color cosmetics.

## Brand Promise

Global Beauty. Local Trust.

Emart connects global-standard beauty products with local verification, customer support, faster delivery, and Bangladesh-focused shopping guidance.

## Tone of Voice

Use a tone that is:

- trustworthy
- clean
- premium but friendly
- Bangladesh-aware
- skincare-guidance focused
- not overclaiming
- not fake medical/dermatology authority

Avoid exaggerated claims such as:

- cures acne
- removes pigmentation permanently
- dermatologist approved, unless verified
- guaranteed original without supporting process

Prefer safer wording:

- helps support
- suitable for
- carefully curated
- authenticity verified
- Bangladesh-friendly guidance
- local support

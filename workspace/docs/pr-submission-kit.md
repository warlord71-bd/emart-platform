## Press Release (copy-paste to all PR sites)

**Title:** Emart Skincare Bangladesh Expands Authentic Korean & Global Beauty Catalog to 3,600+ Products

**Summary:** Emart Skincare Bangladesh (e-mart.com.bd) now offers over 3,600 authenticated skincare products from Korea, Japan, and global brands with nationwide cash-on-delivery.

**Body:**

Dhaka, Bangladesh — Emart Skincare Bangladesh (https://e-mart.com.bd), a dedicated online skincare retailer, has expanded its catalog to over 3,600 verified products spanning Korean, Japanese, and international beauty brands.

The platform carries popular K-beauty brands including COSRX, Beauty of Joseon, SKIN1004, Some By Mi, and Innisfree, alongside dermatologist-recommended lines like CeraVe, La Roche-Posay, and The Ordinary. All products are sourced through verified channels and checked for authenticity before dispatch.

"Bangladesh's skincare market is growing rapidly, especially among younger consumers who want access to the same products available in Seoul or Tokyo," said the Emart team. "We built this platform to make authentic global skincare accessible with local trust — Bengali language support, cash on delivery nationwide, and fast Dhaka delivery."

Key features of the platform include:
- 3,600+ authenticated skincare products
- Korean, Japanese, and global beauty brands
- Cash on delivery across all of Bangladesh
- Dhaka delivery in 1-2 business days
- Outside Dhaka delivery in 3-5 business days
- Product guides and skincare education content

The platform is accessible at https://e-mart.com.bd and serves customers across all 64 districts of Bangladesh.

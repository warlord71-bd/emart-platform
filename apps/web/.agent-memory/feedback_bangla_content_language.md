# Bangla content language preference

Owner preference recorded 2026-06-24:

- Use natural, simple Bangla for mass readers.
- Familiar English skincare terms may remain inside Bangla when the translated Bangla word sounds
  forced, uncommon, or harder to understand.
- Keep English mixing minimal and consistent; use it for clarity, not decoration.
- Prefer short sentences, common vocabulary, direct answers, and mobile-friendly paragraphs.
- Preserve SEO terms naturally, but never force an awkward exact translation or keyword repetition.

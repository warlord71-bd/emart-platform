# Homepage/global SEO title policy

Date: 2026-05-16

Owner-approved homepage/global SEO brand title:

`Emart Skincare Bangladesh | Authentic Korean, Japanese & Global Beauty`

Use this exact wording for the homepage title, homepage OpenGraph title, global metadata fallback, and search-facing brand/tagline surfaces such as WebSite JSON-LD.

Do not paste the full title into every product, brand, category, or article page. Those pages should keep specific intent-led titles, usually ending with the shorter `| Emart` or another existing local pattern, to avoid duplicate/overlong titles and title stuffing.
